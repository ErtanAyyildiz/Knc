Environment

  • We implement an environmentally friendly production model that reduces carbon emissions to protect the environment.
  • Our packagings are 100% recyclable.
  • By producing Oxo-Biogradable packagings, we are able to biodegrade the plastic quickly in nature, leaving a protected environment for the next centuries.

Community

  • We support to the groups and people in need of help by carrying out joint projects with non-governmental organizations

Workplace

  • We care about the training and development of our employees, and contribute to their development through professional consulting services, both in-house and from third parties.
  • We implement activities and programs improving the working environment which increase the wealth and happiness of our employees.

Customers

  • Transparency is our main and essential principle to our customers
  • We offer our customers high-quality and innovative packages which adds value to their sales and brand recognition. We introduce our customers new technologies which contributes to their development.
